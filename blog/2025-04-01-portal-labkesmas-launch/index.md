We are thrilled to announce the launch of the Portal Labkesmas, a comprehensive platform designed to enhance the health laboratory services provided by Labkesmas Magelang. This new portal aims to streamline various processes, improve user experience, and provide better access to laboratory services for both public and internal users.
<!-- truncate -->
## Key Features of Portal Labkesmas

### Services
In this initial launch phase, the Portal Labkesmas offers a range of services to internal users, with plans to expand public services in the near future. The current features include:
#### Internal Services
- SK Download / SK Lending History
- Url Shortener Service
- Procedure Standard Operating (SOP) Management

### On Going Developments
During the initial launch phase, we are focusing on the development and integration of several key modules to further enhance the portal's functionality:
#### Internal Services
- Registration Module
    - Dynamic List of Examination Types
    - Listing Examination Prices
- Attendance Module
#### Public Services
- Juragan Module
    - Clinic Examination Registration
    - Laboratory Examination Registration
    - Laboratory Examination Tracking
    - Scientific Tour Registration
    - Scientific Tour Tracking
    - Sample Shipment Registration
    - Sample Examination Tracking
    

We invite everyone to explore the new Portal Labkesmas and take advantage of the enhanced services it offers. Your feedback is valuable to us as we continue to improve and expand our services.
